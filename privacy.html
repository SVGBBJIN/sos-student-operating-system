Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our Study App ("the App"), hosted at https://svgbbjin.github.io, collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our Google integration features.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize the App via Google OAuth, we access the following data:

  • Google Profile & Email: To identify you and save your preferences.
  • Google Calendar Data: To sync your study schedules and deadlines.
  • Google Docs Content: To create new study notes and summarize your existing documents.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data strictly to provide the core features of the App, specifically:

  • Reading calendar events to help you organize study sessions.
  • Writing to Google Docs to save summaries and notes generated by the App.

3. Google Limited Use Disclosure

The App's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do not: Sell your data, use your data for advertising, or allow humans to read your document content unless it is necessary for security or required by law.

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not store your Google Document or Calendar content on our servers. Processing happens in real-time to generate summaries, and the resulting notes are saved directly back to your Google Drive.
